Travel to Mexico

Mexico is a beautiful place that has
mountains, deserts, jungles and beaches,
making it one of the 10 most megadiverse
countries in the world.



One of the most famous and recommended beaches in Mexico are:

  1. Cozumel
  2. Cancun
  3. Playa del Carmen
  4. Puerto vallarta


It is also known for its culture and it is home
for more than 200 native languages, such as:

  • Tzeltal Maya
  • Tzotzil Maya
  • Mixtec
  • Zapotec
